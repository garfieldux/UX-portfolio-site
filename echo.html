My work in UX design

Echo

Objective: Create an application facilitate live music events by connecting musicians and event organizers

"What is the problem?"

The modern music professional faces a challenge in using online services to facilitate live events.

  • Established websites seem to be confusing experiences because of how many features litter the page
  • Connecting with venues and show organizers is fragmented across email, phone calls, and in-person sign-ups

Musicians need a straight-forward and simple way to find and connect to live show opportunities and show organizers need a straight-forward and simple way to hire talent for their events.
